---
title: "Rivers of living water"
description: "An invitation to drink from the spring that never runs dry."
reference: "John 7:37-38"
pubDate: "2026-07-02"
---

"Whoever believes in me, as Scripture has said, rivers of living water will flow from within them."

Jesus invites us to come to Him to quench our deepest thirst. Not a spring that runs dry, but rivers flowing from within to bless others.

**To pray today:** Lord, fill me with your living Word and make me a channel of your life to those around me.
